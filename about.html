Acerca de Nosotros

Más de 10 años de experiencia en administración de franquicias.

We are a company dedicated to providing administration services to restaurant franchises located in the United States, including services and departments such as Accounting, Payroll, Systems Support, Repair Support and Maintenance.

We wish to project the seriousness that 10 years of experience have given us in the management and administration of restaurants.

Launched in 2020, XB Solutions is a spinoff of an existing business that has been operational since 2010. We provide restaurant franchisees with a complete back office solution for a fraction of the cost of their existing operation.

Based in Phoenix, AZ with our servicing center based in Puerto Penasco “Rocky Point,” Mexico.

Currently works with 45 team members and scalable to fit each client’s needs and servicing over 385 QSRs across 18 states.
