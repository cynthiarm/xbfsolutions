About Us

Launched in 2020, XB Franchise Solutions, LLC is a spinoff of an existing business that has been operational since 2010. With 50+ team members and growing, we provide a full back office solution for U.S based restaurateurs, private equity firms and general business entrepreneurs. From daily accounting to payroll services, we have you covered!

We believe our model of providing a comprehensive back office solution and/or “al a carte” services is highly attractive to growing companies. If something is working well, no need to change it.
