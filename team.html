Meet Team

Meet our board & executive team.

Marcus Di Fiore – President & CEO

Prior to joining XB, Marcus spent the last 20-years in Commercial Banking with the likes of Northern Trust, Wells Fargo, US Bank and Comerica. Marcus originated, underwrote and managed loans to middle market customers with revenues up to $500 million across the following industries: Restaurant, Real Estate, Consumer Products, Apparel, Healthcare, Logistics, Aerospace, and Consumer Services.

Marcus’s expertise includes financing business acquisitions, dividend recaps, real estate projects, working capital facilities, ESOPS, SBA projects, and equipment purchases.

Education: BBA in Accounting from Texas Southern University, Houston, Texas.

Lived and worked in Houston, Chicago, Phoenix, Las Vegas, and San Diego.

Lastly, is the founder of the Encinitas Baseball Club 2025 which is a 14u program designed to prepare players for high school and college baseball. He also chair the finance committee for Mainly Mozart and he is very involved in the nonprofit community.
